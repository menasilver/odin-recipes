Grilled Chicken Recipe

Recipe Description

Today, we are going to learn how to make a Classic Healthy Meal! Grilled Chicken!

Grilled Chicken is a simple, protein packed, and super healthy dish. It is very filling, low calorie and today we will be showing you how to do it step by step!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Grab two pieces of chicken breast
  2. Season with salt & pepper
  3. Add olive oil to a pan and place on medium heat for 5 minutes
  4. Add chicken breasts to pan and flip every 4 minutes
  5. Use thermometer to check if chicken on thickest side is at 165 degrees
  6. Remove and place on cuttin board
  7. Slice chicken and then place onto serving plate
  8. Enjoy!
