Grilled Cheese Recipe

Recipe Description

Today, we are going to learn how to make a Staple American Snack! Grilled Cheese!

Grilled Cheese is a simple, yet delicious, snack that is extremely popular for young kids and adults alike! It can be served alone or with a hearty cup of tomato soup and today we will be showing you how to do it step by step!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Bread
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Tomato Soup

Steps

  1. Grab two pieces of bread
  2. Place Cheese in the middle
  3. Place Tomatoes in the middle
  4. Place Sandwich into a toaster
  5. After 5 minutes remove and slice in half
  6. Open up a can of tomato soup
  7. Heat up in the microwave and serve with the Grilled Cheese Sandwich
  8. Enjoy!
